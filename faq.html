Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about planning, pricing, and how Travel Mate works.

Do you handle international visas and documents?

We provide checklists and guidance. Visa processing is handled by you or an authorized agent—we’ll point you to requirements for your route.

What is included in the trip quote?

Quotes typically outline stays, transfers where applicable, and major inclusions. Exact items depend on the package—you’ll see a line-by-line summary before you commit.

Can I change dates after requesting a booking?

Yes, in most cases. Share new dates as early as possible; availability and fare rules may affect the final price.

Is payment taken on this website?

No. This demo collects trip details only. In production, payment would go through a secure gateway or invoice linked from your team.
